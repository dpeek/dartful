This blog documents my experiences learning [Dart][dart], getting to know the [language][spec], [libraries][core], [toolchain][editor], and [community][community]. Setup and Research

 Monday, 18 February 2013

The [documentation][install] of the Dart website for installation and setup is great, and since I didn't have any problems I don't have much to add. In no time at all, I was skimming through the tutorials, playing with futures from dart:async and writting my first HelloWorld HttpServer with rikulo. Rikulo introduced the idea of a project build.dart where I could define build tasks, A good place to start roughing out the peices of my blog engine.
